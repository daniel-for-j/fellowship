About BHUSF

The Bingham University Student Fellowship is an interdenominational gathering of Christian youths of Bingham University, with the sole purpose of worship, praying and praising God irrespective of denomination. The fellowship started in September 2008 and was inaugurated by the first coordinating University Chaplain Rev. Oludare Aliu

The doctrines of the fellowship shall be based on;

  • The divine inspiration of the word of God.
  • The unity of the Father, Son and Holy Spirit in the Godhead, and their co-equality in power and glory.
  • The sovereignty of God in the creation, revelation, His worthiness to be the object of individual worship and the redemption of mankind (1Tim 2:5, Re. 4:11).
  • The universal sinfulness of man since his fall through disobedience, rendering him guilty and subject to Godâ€™s wrath, judgement and condemnation (Gen 3:1-24, Rom 3:23).

The major aim of the Fellowship is to gather every students under one umbrella to worship and serve God with youthful strength, enhancing their vibrancy as a fellowship together as one body. Hence, the Fellowship was born out of the need:

  • To give Bingham University Students who had not encountered God an experience of Him.
  • To strengthen the spiritual life of those who already had an experience of God.
  • To seek Gods face as a family and as one body.

VISION: Raising Bingham University Students to have an understanding of God and to discover the purpose for which they were created.

MISSION STATEMENT: To carefully and prayerfully empower Bingham University students with the realization of the excellence they have in Christ Jesus through planning and structuring programs and activities that create a friendly atmosphere which will facilitate spiritual growth, and then to have an experience of surpassing consciousness that Jesus is Lord.

Activities of the fellowship are weekly are as follows;

  • Wednesday: a time to pray for pressing and essential needs.

    Time: 6:30pm-8:00pm

  • Friday: a time to fellowship together as one body with programs such as Bible studies, Bible quiz, teachings and talk shows.

    Time: 6:00pm - 8:00pm

For the effective and practical administration of the BHUSF, the structure of the BHUSF shall consist of:

  • The President
  • The Vice President/ Welfare Coordinator
  • The General Secretary
  • The Assistant General Secretary
  • The Financial Secretary
  • The Treasurer
  • The Prayer and Evangelism Secretary
  • The Adult Sunday School Superintendent
  • The Choir Director
  • The Chief Usher
