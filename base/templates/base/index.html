About Us

The Bingham University Student Fellowship

Is an interdenominational gathering of Christian youths of Bingham University, with the sole purpose of worship, praying and praising God irrespective of denomination.

Read More

Mission Statement

To carefully and prayerfully empower Bingham University students with the realization of the excellence they have in Christ Jesus

Vision

Raising Bingham University Students to have an understanding of God and to discover the purpose for which they were created.
